About the Project

Where misunderstanding creates distance, dialogue can build a bridge.

Bridges, Not Walls explores how conflict can become an opportunity for dialogue, empathy, and mutual understanding. Through non-formal learning and intercultural exchange, young people from 6 countries developed practical skills in communication, mediation, active listening, and non-violent conflict resolution.

Croatia Spain Romania Ukraine Moldova Armenia
🎯

Goal

Equip young people with practical conflict-resolution and mediation tools applicable across cultures and communities.

🛠️

Method

Non-formal education — interactive workshops, role-play simulations, empathy circles, and intercultural evenings.

✨

Result

Participants left with personal toolkits, action plans for local peer-mediation programs, and lasting cross-border connections.

What Happened

Activities & Highlights

🛠️
Workshop

Conflict Resolution Workshops

Hands-on sessions where participants explored conflict styles, mapped causes and triggers, and practised structured dialogue techniques.

⚖️
Simulation

Mediation Arena

Real-world role-play scenarios where participants rotated as mediators, disputants, and observers — building empathy from every angle.

🌍
Cultural Night

Intercultural Evenings

Each national team hosted an interactive evening of traditional food, music, dance, and stories — celebrating diversity across 6 cultures.

💬
Skills Lab

Nonviolent Communication

Participants learned to transform accusatory language into "I-messages," practising precision and empathy in conflict conversations.

🏛️
Study Visit

Osijek City Game

A full-day exploration of Osijek's multicultural heritage — walking tours, resident interviews, and challenges across squares and alleys.

🎨
Co-creation

#BridgesNotWalls Campaign

Mixed-nationality teams created videos, posters, and interactive tools — building an online conflict-resolution resource hub.

What We Learned

Key Topics

🤝

Conflict Resolution

Understanding conflict types, stages, and constructive approaches to transform disagreements into opportunities.

⚖️

Mediation

Structured negotiation with a neutral third party — 6-stage process, mediator profile, real-world practice.

👂

Active Listening

Listening Relay exercises, Empathy Circles — paying full attention to understand what others say and feel.

💬

Nonviolent Communication

NVC principles and I-messages — expressing needs without blame to open dialogue and reduce tension.

❤️

Emotional Intelligence

Emotional Triggers Lab, Feelings Inventory — recognising and regulating emotions as a foundation for empathy.

🌍

Intercultural Dialogue

Cross-cultural collaboration, challenging stereotypes, and building bridges across 6 different national contexts.
